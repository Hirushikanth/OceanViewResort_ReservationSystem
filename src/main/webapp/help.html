How to Book a Room

1. Create an account or Log in.
2. Click "New Reservation" on your dashboard.
3. Select your dates and preferred room type.
4. Submit your request. Our staff will review availability and confirm your booking shortly.

pending_actions

Check Booking Status

You can track the status of your reservation directly from your Dashboard.

Pending: Awaiting staff confirmation.
Confirmed: Your room is assigned and ready!

receipt_long

View Your Bill

Once your booking is Confirmed, a "Bill" button will appear next to your reservation in the dashboard history. Click it to view or print your official invoice.
